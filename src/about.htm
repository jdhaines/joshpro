About JoshPro LLC

   I want to personally welcome you to the website for JoshPro LLC.  My name is Josh Haines and I am the owner.  I started this small business in October of 2009 as a way to turn my hobby of working with computers into something a little more legitimate.

   I have enjoyed working with computers for many years, beginning in college. From there, I learned how to build PCs from scratch in order to get exactly the components that I wanted.  Friends and family members began asking me to build them computers when they saw the benefits received and value gained from custom PCs.

   Towards the end of my time in college I began teaching myself the code required to build websites.  It began with a couple of small hobby sites and turned into another outlet for my hobby.  I started on a few small sights for customers and have slowly progressed from there.  I'm still very much learning, and attempt to grow my abilities with each new project.  The rest, as they say, is history.
